What's LEIDIA

LEIDIA is a project dedicated to exploring the intersection of digital art and textures inspired by leather. Through various artistic mediums, we aim to transform the rich and tactile feeling of leather into stunning visual compositions. Our work delves into the intricate patterns, subtle color variations, and unique character of this material, reinterpreting it in a modern, digital context.
